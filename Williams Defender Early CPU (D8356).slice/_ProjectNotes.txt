Warning: This board has +12v and -5v supplies that are used to power the 4116 RAM. There is also an unregulated +12v used in the reset circuit.

If this board has been adapted to use 4164 RAM using an adaptor or other means then these +12v and -5v supplies will not be present but the +12v unregulated supply will always be present.

Ensure that the test clip and ground do not come into contact with any of these locations or damage to the Digilent unit may occur.
